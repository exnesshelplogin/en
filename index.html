How To Login with Exness

Getting into your Exness account is the first part of managing your trading and accessing tools, including lots of tools. Here’s how you get going with Exness Login:

  • Visit the main Exness site
  • Select “Sign In” and click it at the top right corner
  • Provide your email that you registered with. Provide your password.

If you're using the Exness App, Exness Login is really without fuss with the same action as the website. If you have your 2FA applied, there will be a code sent to your authentication device to allow you to log in.

You wish not to download anything? Exness has their Web Terminal; this means you do not need to download anything; it simply requires a browser on any modern device.

Changing Trading Terminal Inside Exness

Exness allows for multiple trading terminals for traders after Exness Login is complete. For example, you can gain access to MetaTrader 4, MetaTrader 5, and of course, their web terminal. Switching between them is just that easy. Here's how:

  • Login to your Exness account
  • Go the Trading Accounts
  • Select the account and chose the terminal you wish to use
  • Open the platform

Whether you are located in Dubai or anywhere else, you can change terminal to suit your personal trading style and technical preferences.
